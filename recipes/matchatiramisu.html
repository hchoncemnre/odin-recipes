Matcha Tiramisu

Description

I turned 21 last week! Which means, it's time for a cake recipe! I made matcha tiramisu this year. Enjoy the recipe :)

Ingredients

Matcha Ladyfingers

  • 5g Cornstarch
  • 55g Cake/Pastry Flour
  • 5g Matcha Powder
  • 2g Salt
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 20g Sugar
  • 40g Sugar
  • 2 Egg Whites

Dipping Solution

  • 50g Water
  • 8g Sugar
  • 3g Instant Coffee Cowder

Tiramisu Base

  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 60g Sugar
  • 2g Vanilla Extract
  • 150g Mascarpone Cheese
  • 100g Whipping Cream

Instructions

Matcha Ladyfingers

  1. Whip the egg whites with 40g of sugar until soft peak
  2. In another bowl, beat the yolks with 20g of sugar and vanilla until pale yellow and smooth
  3. Fold the whites together with the yolk mixture until smooth
  4. Sift the baking powder, cake flour, and cornstarch into the folded egg mixture. Fold gently and evenly
  5. Preheat the oven for 10–15 minutes at 340ºF
  6. Pipe biscuit mixture into desired long bars. Cover with a thin layer of powdered sugar
  7. After baking is complete, put the cake in the oven and leave it in for 1 hour at 180ºF

Tiramisu Base

  1. Place a bowl of yolk on a pot of hot water, mix with sugar and vanilla extract until 70ºC
  2. Mix egg yolk mixture with soften mascarpone cheese, then add 50% of whipped cream

Final Assemble

  1. Soak ladyfingers in coffee liquid, put them in your container, top with cream
  2. Sprinkle with plenty matcha powder
  3. Repeat until desired. Leave in the fridge for at least 4 hours before consume
