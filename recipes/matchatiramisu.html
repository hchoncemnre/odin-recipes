Matcha Tiramisu

Description

I turned 21 last week! Which means, it's time for a cake recipe! I decided to make tiramisu this year, but spiced it up with a matcha flavour. Enjoy the recipe :)

Ingredients

Matcha Lady Fingers

  • 5g Cornstarch
  • 55g Cake/Pastry Flour
  • 5g Matcha Powder
  • 2g Salt
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 20g Sugar
  • 40g Sugar
  • 2 Egg Whites

Dipping Solution

  • 50g Water
  • 8g Sugar
  • 3g Instant Coffee Cowder

Tiramisu Base

  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 60g Sugar
  • 2g Vanilla Extract
  • 150g Mascarpone Cheese
  • 100g Whipping Cream
