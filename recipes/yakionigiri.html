Yaki Onigiri

Description

Perfect food to pack for a roadtrip! :)

Ingredients

Rice & Seasoning

  • Cooked sushi rice
  • Salt
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Mirin
  • Furikake (optional)
  • Sesame oil

Filling

  • Tuna
  • Kewpie Mayonnaise
  • Sriracha
  • Black Pepper
  • Chopped sour kimchi

To Grill

  • Teriyaki sauce
  • Cooking oil
  • Seaweed sheet

Instructions

Making Rice Balls

  1. Season your rice with ingredients, adjust based on your preferences
  2. Wet your hands so the rice won't stick
  3. Scoop desired amount of rice onto your palm
  4. Put filling in
  5. Cover rice with other hand and shape into triangle

Grilling Rice Balls

  1. Lightly oil a cast-iron skillet on medium heat
  2. Grill onigiri until all sides are crispy
  3. Brush on teriyaki or unagi sauce, turn heat down to medium-low
